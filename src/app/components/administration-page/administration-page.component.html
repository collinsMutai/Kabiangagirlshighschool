Joyce Chumo - Board Chairperson

I am privileged to have led the Board of Management of Kabianga girls’ high school during the time when the Kabianga girls school’s strategic plan (for 2022-2027) was developed. For the board, the strategic plan is the key document for our work in guiding the school and its development on the next five years. Kabianga girls’ school is blessed with assets that contribute to the education and nurturing of the young Kenyan talent. These assets if carefully protected and expanded can serve even more Kenyan girls to reach their full potential not only in academics but also in sports, home science, drama, and music.

The strategic plan lays on the current status of the school, the staffing, and the infrastructure of the basis from a broad set of actionable plans that once implemented position Kabianga girls to be one of the best public High schools in Belgut, Kericho county. The board has great ambitions for Kabianga girls as a center of excellence in academic values and in co-curricular, and as a top academic High school. We are proud that Kabianga girls admits girls from all parts of the country and molds them in four years into talented responsible young girls with lifelong friendship formed between students of diverse background and origins.

On behalf of the Board, I want to thank everyone who engaged directly or indirectly in our strategic planning process. First is the school administration led by the School Principal Mrs. Lucie Kirui; the Board of Management members; the Parent Association members; the teaching and non-teaching staff fraternity; and the students’ community at large among others. These teams provided invaluable information and feedback that helped in ensuring the development of focused and realistic goals that align to the needs and aspirations of the school. They also provided critical leadership during the development process in ensuring breadth of vision and continuity of values and encouraging a truly collaborative, inclusive process.

The strategic five-year plan is ambitious for Kabianga girls with commitment by the Board, Ministry, the school administration, the parents, and all other stake holders including corporate partners, we believe that this plan can lead Kabianga girls High school to new heights of learning and teaching that will benefit many young Kenyan girls. This strategic plan will help in providing a framework and serve as an important guide in helping our school succeed bearing in mind the many national challenges facing our learning institutions. The recognized and opportunities will lay a firm foundation in strengthening our institutional capacity to help meet our goals and strategic objectives.

I am confident that all these goals will help Kabianga girls high school enhance the work environment that will provide a rewarding and rigorous educational experience for our students and conducive working environment to all employees. Your continuous participation will be greatly appreciated.

One thing we know very well is that the church-sponsored schools lead the nation in exam scores. All the top schools in Kenya are the ones where the church is either the sponsor (public schools) or sole proprietor (private schools). We are mandated by the church to oversee the development and success of the schools, mainly to ensure spiritual development of the students.
